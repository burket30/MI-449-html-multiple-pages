My favorite books by the author C.S. Lewis

View List

I never really got into reading books in my free time until recently. The books I've picked up have mostly been from C.S. Lewis plus a couple of others from other authors. Since most of my focus has been on Lewis' books, I thought it would be most appropriate to consider his works only for a favorites list.

Please keep in mind that Lewis' Christian faith and life, and the way these were communicated in his works, are probably what I most respect about the author. If you aren't a Christian yourself, don't worry if any of my comments don't seem to make much sense.
